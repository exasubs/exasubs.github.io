BD Donation Drive – March

March 10, 2016 at 9:01 pm

We’ve delivered on our promise of releasing a volume of Blurays every weekday until 2016 ends. We’ve released 54 volumes so far and need 207 to reach our goal on the 30th December 2016. We’ve released 4 extra volumes so far in case we miss a day here or there in future. We’ve ran into a problem, however. Some of the Fall 2015 Blurays we intended to pick up are still not uploaded and we’re unable to work on them. We’ve waited three months for raws to possibly surface, but at this point of time we have no choice but to order the BDs and rip them ourselves.

Hence, I would like to appeal to all our Bluray downloaders to consider donating a couple of dollars towards funding the purchase of Blurays or sharing the BDs if you already own copies of the volumes listed. We need copies of:

Ore Monogatari 1|2|3|4|5|6|7|8
Sakurako-san no Ashimoto ni wa Shitai ga Umatteiru 1|2|3|4|5|6
selector spread WIXOSS 1|2|3
Subete ga F ni Naru BD-Box
Taimadou Gakuen 35 Shiken Shoutai 1|2|3|4|5|6
Working!!! 3|4|5|6|7|8

These all add up to roughly around $2100 worth of BDs in the unfortunate event that we have to buy all of them ourselves. If you would like to donate towards a particular series, please do let me know in the comments. Also, we’ll be able to see your email in the email field so there’s no need to copypaste it into the comments field unless you have a different email from the gravatar email. We take both paypal and Amazon USA giftcards. Thank you for donating!
